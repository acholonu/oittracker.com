# Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 10, 2026

**Last Updated:** July 12, 2026

This Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy describes how Jones Technical Enterprises, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares consumer health data through the OIT Tracker mobile application (the "App"). It supplements our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy) and applies to consumer health data protected by applicable U.S. state consumer-health-data laws.

OIT Tracker is a tracking and record-organization tool for families managing oral immunotherapy. Although the App does not provide healthcare or replace a healthcare professional, the information it records can reveal a person's health conditions, symptoms, treatment, medication use, and treatment progress. We therefore treat the information described below as consumer health data.

---

We will not collect, use, or share additional categories of consumer health data, or use existing consumer health data for materially different purposes, without updating this policy and obtaining affirmative consent when required by law.

## 2. Sources of Consumer Health Data

We collect consumer health data from the following categories of sources:

- **Parents and guardians:** Information they enter, scan, import, save, or authorize for a child.
- **Authorized Minors age 13 or older and adult caregivers:** Information they enter after receiving access from a parent, guardian, or family owner. Children under 13 may not use the App or enter information directly.
- **The App and device:** App activity, device and App information, identifiers, timestamps, and product telemetry generated when the App is used.
- **Documents supplied by users:** Information contained in emergency action plans or other material a user chooses to scan, import, or enter.
- **Healthcare professionals or clinics, indirectly:** Information a user copies from a clinician, clinic, prescription, treatment plan, or action-plan document. We do not obtain this information directly from the professional or clinic.
- **Information we derive:** Treatment-progress metrics calculated from dose logs and other information already supplied through the App.
- **Apple:** The Apple-issued sign-in identifier, optional email address, and subscription status used to authenticate the account and provide subscribed features associated with health records.

We do not collect consumer health data about your activities over time across unaffiliated websites or online services.

## 3. Consumer Health Data We Share

We do not sell consumer health data. We do not share consumer health data for targeted advertising.

Depending on the features a user chooses, we disclose or make available the following categories of consumer health data:

| Recipient Category | Recipient | Consumer Health Data Disclosed | Purpose |
|--------------------|-----------|---------------------------------|---------|
| Cloud hosting and infrastructure processor | Google Cloud Platform | Account-linked consumer health data, saved health records, derived metrics, product telemetry, and transient action-plan parsing requests and responses | Host, secure, process, and synchronize the App and its records |
| Artificial-intelligence processing provider | Google Cloud Platform, through Vertex AI (using Google's Gemini model and, as a fallback, Anthropic's Claude model available through Vertex AI) | The action-plan page image you submit for AI auto-fill, and the structured extraction response; processed transiently, not used to train models, and not retained after the request is served (other than limited, disable-able security logging) | Convert an action-plan image into structured fields for you to review, only when you request AI auto-fill |
| Transactional email processor | Amazon Web Services, through SES | A caregiver recipient's email address and invitation code; no child name or treatment record is included in the invitation email | Deliver an invitation requested by the account owner |
| People selected by the user | Invited adult caregivers, co-owners, or an Authorized Minor age 13 or older | Child profile and treatment information, dose history, reactions, notes, sickness status, schedules, saved action-plan information, clinician contacts, and care instructions, subject to the role and visibility settings selected by the owner | Provide the family-sharing feature requested by the owner |
| Government, regulatory, or legal recipients | Courts, regulators, law enforcement, or other recipients legally entitled to the information | Only the consumer health data legally required in the circumstances | Comply with valid legal process or other applicable law |

We do not currently share consumer health data with any affiliate. Apple receives account authentication and subscription information but does not receive dose logs, reactions, action-plan content, or other treatment records from us. Kit receives waitlist email addresses collected on the website but does not receive App consumer health data from us.

When a family owner grants another person access, that person may be able to view, add, change, download, print, or otherwise retain consumer health data according to the permissions selected. Revoking access prevents future access through our systems but cannot recall information the person already viewed or retained.

## 4. Your Consumer Health Data Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

- Confirm whether we are collecting, sharing, or selling consumer health data concerning you or a child for whom you are authorized to act.
- Access that consumer health data.
- Review and correct inaccurate consumer health data through the App or by contacting us.
- Request a list of third parties and affiliates with whom we shared or sold the consumer health data, including available contact information.
- Withdraw consent to future collection or sharing of consumer health data when processing is based on consent. Withdrawal does not affect processing that occurred before withdrawal and may prevent us from providing an optional feature that requires the data.
- Request deletion of consumer health data from our records, including applicable archived and backup systems, and ask us to notify processors, contractors, and other recipients required to honor the request.
- Appeal a refusal to act on a consumer health data request.

A parent or legal guardian may exercise these rights for their child. We may take reasonable steps to verify the requester's identity, authority, and relationship to the data. We will not require you to create a new account to submit a request, but we may ask you to use an existing account or provide information reasonably necessary to authenticate the request.

### How to Submit a Request

Email **support@oittracker.com** with the subject line **Consumer Health Data Request** and state which right you want to exercise. You may also delete your account and account-linked records through **Settings → Account → Delete Account** in the App. Please do not send health records or identity documents by unencrypted email unless we specifically ask you to use an approved secure method.

You can revoke a person's family-sharing access from the Account screen. You can stop future product telemetry from **Settings → Privacy & Data** by switching off the setting currently labeled **Share Anonymous Analytics**. AI action-plan auto-fill occurs only when you turn on and consent to that feature for a document. Contact us using the request method above if you want to withdraw consent more broadly or need help using these controls.

We will respond without undue delay and ordinarily within 45 days after receiving the request. When reasonably necessary, we may extend the response period once by an additional 45 days and will explain the extension during the initial response period. Access information is provided without charge up to twice per year, except where applicable law permits a reasonable fee or refusal for manifestly unfounded, excessive, or repetitive requests.

Deletion from an archived or backup system may be delayed when permitted by law, but no longer than legally allowed. Deletion cannot remove information from a device, printout, export, screenshot, or other copy retained independently by a person with whom the user previously shared it.

### Appeals

If we refuse to act on your request, email **support@oittracker.com** with the subject line **Consumer Health Data Appeal** and explain why you believe the decision should be reconsidered. We will respond to the appeal in writing within 45 days. If we deny the appeal, our response will explain the decision and provide a method for contacting the appropriate state attorney general to submit a complaint.

We will not unlawfully discriminate against you for exercising consumer health data rights.
